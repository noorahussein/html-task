TOYOTA

Toyota Motor Corporation (Japanese: トヨタ自動車株式会社) is a Japanese multinational automobile manufacturer headquartered in Toyota, Aichi, Japan. In 2017, Toyota's corporate structure consisted of 364,445 employees worldwide[7] and as of September 2018, it was the sixth largest company in the world by revenue.

Mercedes

Mercedes-Benz (German: Mercedes-Benz); is a German global automotive brand and a subsidiary of Daimler. Mercedes-Benz is known for its luxury vehicles, vans, trucks, buses and ambulances. It is headquartered in Stuttgart in the state of Baden-Württemberg. The brand first appeared in 1926 when Daimler-Benz was born. In 2018, Mercedes-Benz was the best-selling luxury car brand in the world with 2.31 million vehicles sold. The name first appeared in 1926 during the reign of Daimler-Benz..
